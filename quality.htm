Our Quality Policy


Committed to quality, is the key behind our success. Asia Steel Tubes Industries is an ISO 9001:2000 Company. We have wide variety of sophisticated manufacturing facilities with the likes of Cutting machines, Power Presses, Drilling machines , Milling machines (both vertical and horizontal).

We have been following all the norms for maintaining quality in all the processes done. Testing after each process is a must in our company. We have a Quality Audit and Assurance department which ensures all the activities to be Quality Oriented. We have adequate tools and instruments to take all the measures for implementing the quality standards.
