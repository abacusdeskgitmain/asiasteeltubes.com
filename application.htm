Application


The pipes produced by ASTI are variantly applicable for wide market segment in automobiles, steel plants, fan industry, furnitures, heavy engineering industry etc. ASTI is currently focussing on automobiles and fan industries in domestic and international market. The lancing pipes helps in opening the jammed tap-holes, cleaning of spills from continuous caster or simply for any buring operation. The tubes are also applicable in small operations like drilling, bending, threading or "used as oxygen lancers and blast furnaces".

The product spectrum covers all sorts of tubes and pipes made out of steel. These precision tubes are very useful for the engineering industries such as used for the accessories of automobiles after plating or for bending, flairing, flattening and crimping operation. It can also be used for the manufacturing of bushes, shafts and other engineering items.

Burning Lances can be employed to cut all types of steel, metals i.e. copper, steel, cast steel, cast iron, skull, heavy melting scrap and for Ferro Alloys Plants.
