Stainless steel pipes are used in oil, petrochemical, refinery and process related industries. Stainless steel is an alloy of iron having more than 10% of chromium.

Iron steel pipes are used in transportation and distribution of water. Iron steel pipes possess a very high tensile strength which makes it an ultimate choice as a material for manufacturing pipes.

Black steel pipes are used for line pipes in oil and petroleum industries, for water wells and for water, gas and sewage purposes. Black steel pipe gets its appearance because of a black oxide scale formed on the surface of steel pipes when they are forged.
